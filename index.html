ABOUT ME

Jie Lin received his B.Eng degree in Microelectronics Science and Engineering from Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech), Shenzhen, China, in 2016. From 2013 to 2015, he was with Prof. Hongyu Yu's group in SUSTech and was focusing on AlGaN/GaN high electron mobility transistor (HEMT) and its reliability. From October 2015, he was with Prof. Chenchang Zhan's group in SUSTech as a research assistant and teaching assistant.

Now he is in the State Key Laboratory of Analog and Mixed-Signal VLSI, University of Macau, and he is currently working on the joint Ph.D. program supervised by Prof. Yan Lu (U Macau) and Prof. Chenchang Zhan (SUSTech).

His research interests include wireless power link, DC-DC converter and low dropout regulator.
